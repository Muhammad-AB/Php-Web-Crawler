Jessica Mathews Jessica Mathews is a senior writer for  Fortune  covering venture capital, private equity, startups, and M&A. She writes  Term Sheet , a daily dealmaking newsletter.  Before joining  Fortune , she covered wealth management and custody as a reporter at  Financial Planning  magazine. Jessica was a 2018 Dow Jones News Fund intern, and she interned at CNBC in 2017. She is a graduate from The King’s College in New York City, where she was the editor-in-chief of her student newspaper, the  Empire State Tribune .
