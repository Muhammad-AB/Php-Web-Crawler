Jane Thier Jane Thier is a reporter at  Fortune  on the Success team. Previously, she reported on finance and business news at  Industry Dive , and before that, worked in the newsrooms at  Sauce Magazine , SmartAsset, and Lincoln Center for the Performing Arts. Originally from New York City, Jane graduated from Washington University in St. Louis with majors in English Literature and American Culture Studies.
