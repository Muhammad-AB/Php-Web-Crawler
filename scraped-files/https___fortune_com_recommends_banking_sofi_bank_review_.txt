TITLE: SoFi Bank Review: Checking and savings account rates | Fortune Recommends
SoFi Bank Review 2023: An online bank with solid APYs and a long list of financial services SoFi (which stands for Social Finance, Inc.) began as a student loan refinancing company and has since expanded into a broad financial services company that offers a range of products, including bank accounts, loans, investments, insurance, and more. SoFi operates primarily online and does not have physical branches. SoFi customers have access to a network of more than 55,000 ATMs nationwide. Customers can pay bills, find ATMs, send money, make mobile deposits, and more via SoFi’s website and mobile app. All rates and fees are current as of October 31, 2023, and are subject to change.
