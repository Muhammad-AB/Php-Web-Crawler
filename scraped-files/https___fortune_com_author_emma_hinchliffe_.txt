Emma Hinchliffe Emma Hinchliffe is a senior writer at  Fortune , where she covers women in business and gender issues across business, politics, and culture. Emma is the author of  The Broadsheet ,  Fortune ’s daily newsletter for and about the world’s most powerful women; a co-chair of Fortune’s  Most Powerful Women Summit  and  Most Powerful Women Next Gen Summit ; and a co-chair of the  Fortune /U.S. Department of State Global Women’s Mentoring Partnership. Before joining  Fortune , she worked as a reporter for  Mashable  and the  Houston Chronicle . She is a graduate of Georgetown University.
