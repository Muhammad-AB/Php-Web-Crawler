Paige McGlauflin Paige McGlauflin is a reporter on  Fortune 's leadership desk who writes the  CHRO Daily  newsletter. Previously, Paige was a contributing writer with Seattle Gay News and a reporter and editorial assistant with Paperwork Studios, a New York City-based media startup that produced B2B newsletters. At Paperwork, she covered diversity, equity, and inclusion in the technology space; investor relations in private equity and venture capital; and community banking.
