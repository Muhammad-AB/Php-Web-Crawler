Jeff John Roberts Jeff John Roberts is the editor of Fortune Crypto, overseeing coverage of blockchain and how technology is changing finance. He also writes news, features and the daily  Fortune Crypto  newsletter. A former attorney, Jeff has also written for  Reuters , the  New York Times , the  Globe&Mail  and  The Economist .  He is the author of  Kings of Crypto , a critically-acclaimed account of Coinbase.
