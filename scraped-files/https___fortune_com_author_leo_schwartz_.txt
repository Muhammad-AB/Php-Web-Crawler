Leo Schwartz Leo Schwartz is a reporter at  Fortune  covering the global impact of crypto, regulation, cyber crime, and all things blockchain. He writes  Proof of State , the Wednesday edition of the daily Fortune Crypto newsletter. He previously worked at Rest of World, covering tech from Mexico City. His work has also appeared in the  Washington Post , PBS NewsHour, and  The Nation . He graduated with a dual master's in journalism and Latin American studies from New York University and a bachelor's from Columbia University. Follow all of Leo's stories  here . Got a tip?
